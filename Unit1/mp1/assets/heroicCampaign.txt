Immediately after assassinating Charles Conners, Kevin Dustin barged into the syndicate's headquarters and murdered Metal Grip before a single word could be uttered. The news rapidly spread of the deaths of Governor Conners and the infamous Metal Grip. As soon as the shady deals made by Charles were exposed, the killer was labeled a hero for taking care of the city's two biggest parasites. While being hunted by the remnants of the syndicate, Kevin Dustin adopted the title 'Vindicator', and he wiped out the rest of the criminals in the syndicate for personal vengeance, while the public believed this troubled man was a hero dealing out justice.
