Kevin Dustin was born to a politician, named Charles Conners, and a drug dealer, Bianca Dustin. To protect his career, Senator Conners hid his affair with Bianca, and even sent her away after discovering she was pregnant with his child. Devestated, Bianca fell deeper into her addiction. Due to his mother consuming so many drugs while pregnant with him, Kevin Dustin was born with a disturbed mind, which only became worse as life went on.
